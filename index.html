WaguWagu Survivors

Play as a weapon, master unique abilities, conquer challenging platforms!

About The Game

WaguWagu Survivors is a challenging platformer adventure where you play as a weapon with unique abilities. Navigate through difficult terrain, solve puzzles, and unlock new weapon forms as you progress through this thrilling adventure!

Controls

Control Action
Escape Pause
Mouse movement Look/Aim
Mouse 1 Activate weapon
Mouse 2 Special action (when available)
Mouse wheel Switch weapons

How To Play

Master Your Weapons

Each weapon has unique properties and abilities. Learn to use them effectively to overcome obstacles!

Switch Between Forms

Unlock new weapon forms as you progress. Switch between them to solve puzzles and reach new areas.

Time Your Actions

Precise timing is crucial! Some challenges require you to chain multiple weapon abilities in sequence.

Game Features

Challenging Platforming

Test your skills in a difficult but fair platformer that rewards practice and precision.

Unique Weapon Mechanics

Play as the weapon itself! Each form has different physics and abilities to master.

Metroidvania Elements

Unlock new areas as you gain new weapon forms and abilities. Backtrack to find secrets!

Hidden Secrets

Keep an eye out for secret areas and collectibles throughout the game. Can you find the hidden duck?
